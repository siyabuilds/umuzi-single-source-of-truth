# Getting Started on Slack

## Slack at Umuzi: How We Communicate

At Umuzi, Slack is our primary internal communication tool—keeping us connected, collaborative, and aligned in a remote environment. It’s where we share updates, ask questions, and work together efficiently without cluttering our inboxes.

We use Slack to:
- ✅ Organise discussions in channels for teams, projects, and key topics
- ✅ Keep conversations focused with threads instead of endless message chains
- ✅ Use direct messages (DMs) for quick, one-on-one chats
- ✅ Share files, links, and updates seamlessly
- ✅ Hop on audio or video huddles for quick check-ins
- ✅ Keep things fun with reactions, emojis, and GIFs 🎉

To get the most out of Slack, make sure you’re using the right channels, keeping messages clear, and leveraging features like search, pinned messages, and shortcuts.
