# Adding Images to the Website

The Umuzi website uses a lot of imagery as one of its key elements, and as much as it looks good, it needs to perform as such and not slow the website down or increase load time.  

When adding images to the site, no matter your role (editor/admin), please compress the image down to a smaller size.

## How to Compress Images

Here's how you can do it using this recommended website:

1. Go to `imagecompressor.com`
2. Make sure you're on the **"Compressor"** tab.
3. Click **"Select files"** to upload images or drag your image files to the specified zone.
4. Click **"Compress"**.
5. For each image, adjust the quality slider by reducing it as much as you can while not compromising the quality. Please always aim to get an after image size of below **1 MB**. But use your judgement and don't overdo it.
6. Click the **Apply** button to save the compression.
7. Repeat the steps for all your image files and press the **"Save all"** button above to download the compressed image files.
8. Upload them to wherever you want to use them (i.e., assets folder, CMS, etc.).
