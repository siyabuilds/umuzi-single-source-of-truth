# Google Meet Limits

## Summary
We are on the school Google Workspace plan called **Google for Education Fundamentals**. We have extra licenses for hosting calls with learners called the **Teaching & Learning Upgrade** that give extra functionality and should be used for all Google Meets with learners. This is assigned to the `technical@umuzi.org` account that we should always use to schedule and host learner calls to make sure it has maximum permissions (to track attendance, to record, etc.). LX coaches all have access to this account as well as Tech & Data team members for running learner sessions.

The following accounts also have access to this license upgrade:
- `apply@umuzi.org`
- `general@umuzi.org`
- Specific staff members

As long as a user with the **Teaching & Learning Upgrade** license is the meeting owner, they will have all premium features available with that upgrade (full list [here](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/7317473#max-participants&zippy=%2Cparticipant-limits)). ## Number of Participants Allowed
With the **Teaching & Learning Upgrade**, we are allowed for up to:
- 250 participants per call
- 10,000 viewers in live streams

[More information on participant limits](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/7317473#max-participants&zippy=%2Cparticipant-limits)

## Time Limits
Google Meets calls can be up to **24 hours long** in the Umuzi Education Google Workspace; you don't even need to use an account with the **Teaching & Learning Upgrade**.
