# How to Add, Update, Remove Open Roles

## As an Admin

1. Go to the CMS workflow (top left region).
2. Navigate to the "Open Roles" collection (left region).

### Add an Open Role
- Click "+ New Open Role" (top right region).
- Fill in all the details.
- The background gradient image options can be found and downloaded from the assets or in this Google Drive folder.
- Publish by choosing one of the options under the expanded view found after clicking the down arrow under the "Create draft" button. **Publish now** option is recommended.

### Update an Open Role
1. Follow steps 1 and 2 above to find the list of roles.
2. Select the role you want to update from the list.
3. Make the updates.
4. Publish the role for changes to take place.

### Remove an Open Role
1. Follow steps 1 and 2 above to find the list of roles.
2. Select the role you want to remove.
3. Unpublish the role by choosing the "Unpublish" option under the expanded view found after clicking the down arrow under the "Publish now" button. 
4. Optionally, remove the role entirely by pressing the delete button when you scroll down.
