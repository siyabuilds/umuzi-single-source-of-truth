# How to Use Slab

Welcome to Slab!  
Slab is our go-to platform for organising and sharing knowledge at Umuzi. Whether you're looking for company policies, team processes, or learning resources, Slab helps keep everything in one place and easy to find.

## Getting Started

If you're new to Slab, start by:
- Exploring the sidebar to browse topics.
- Using the search bar to quickly find what you need.
- Checking out key documents pinned for easy access.

## How to Collaborate on Slab

Slab makes it easy to contribute and collaborate. You can:
- ✅ Create and edit documents.
- ✅ Comment on posts to ask questions or give feedback.
- ✅ Tag teammates to get their input.
- ✅ Share links to specific pages for quick reference.
