# Google Classroom Plan & Limits

## Plan
We are on the **Google for Education Fundamentals** plan. You can always double check what is included in our plan [here](https://edu.google.com/workspace-for-education/editions/compare-editions/#compare-editions-table) or by searching on Google.

## Inviting Learners
For inviting learners, you can refer to the following link: [Inviting Learners](https://support.google.com/edu/classroom/answer/6020282?hl=en&authuser=0#outsidedomain&zippy=%2Cinvite-students-with-an-email-invite).

There are 3 options that can be used to invite learners:
- **Send an invite link** — Students click the link to join.
- **Send an email invite** — Students can join from the email or in Classroom.
- **Share a class code** — Students enter the code in Classroom.

> Google is rolling out a change in November 2025 and will not allow Google Classroom invitations by email for learners outside our domain. They can still be invited using links & QR codes but not directly through the invite learners option in Google Classroom.

Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals users can only send email invites within their domain or other trusted domains. To invite students from external domains, share the class code or invite link. 

This does not matter for situations where we are adding learners with Umuzi emails. However, this will impact recruitment bootcamps and pathways without Umuzi email creation, such as Be Green or SAP. People without Umuzi emails accessing Google Classroom will need to set up.

If you need to invite learners outside the Umuzi domain to a Google Classroom, you need to plan to send an email with the Google Classroom information in it (either the link or the code), so they can join the classroom. You can draft your email and submit a Tech request at least 2 business days in advance of when it needs to be sent.

## Daily Limit
There is a limit of **500 invitations per teacher per day**, so we can get around this limit by having multiple teachers within a single classroom when we need to invite more than 500. For more information, visit [Daily Limit](https://support.google.com/edu/classroom/answer/7300976?hl=en).
