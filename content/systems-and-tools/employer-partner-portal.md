# Employer Partner Portal

This manual guides users of the Placements portal application. It is designed to manage candidates who need to be placed in opportunities.

## Logging In

1. Navigate to the landing page: [https://umuzi.retool.com/p/placement-portal](https://umuzi.retool.com/p/placement-portal)
2. Clicking on the “get started” button should redirect you to the list of candidates; otherwise, it will redirect you to the login page if you have not logged in or your session has expired.
3. Enter your credentials on the login form.
4. Click the "Login" button to access the application.

## Candidate Cards

### Overview

Upon logging in, users will see a list of candidate cards.

### Actions

- **View Details**: Click anywhere on a card to view detailed candidate information.
- **Like/Unlike**: Click the like icon to like or unlike a candidate (this demonstrates an interest in the candidate).

## Candidate Details Page

After clicking a card, users are redirected to the candidate details page where they can:

- View comprehensive details about the candidate.
- See whether they have shown interest in the candidate.
- Like or unlike the candidate (show interest in the candidate).

## User Profile

This page enables you to see your information as well as actions you have performed in the portal, such as the candidates that you have liked.

## Sending Messages

Once a candidate is liked, click the "Send to Umuzi" button.

1. A dialog box will appear to type a message of your choice.
2. Click "Send" to email the Umuzi Placements team about the candidate(s) you liked.
