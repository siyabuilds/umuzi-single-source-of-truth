# DataCamp

We have access to a platform called **DataCamp** which offers great online content.  
[DataCamp](https://app.datacamp.com/)  
We currently have access for free for a certain number of licenses through their *DataCamp Donates* programme. 

## Tips for Usage

Before adding learners, please be sure to create a new Team for that cohort of learners, so it's easy to see who each learner is and why they have DataCamp access. Teams also allow you to assign different content to different learners. 

For example, if you are adding the next XPL cohort, make a new team called "XPL AIA Month Year" such as "XPL AIA Nov 25". 

Then go to the learners tab and invite learners to that team. You will be able to publish coursework to that specific group so that other learners don't see irrelevant things.

You can also free up licenses by removing learners at any time.
