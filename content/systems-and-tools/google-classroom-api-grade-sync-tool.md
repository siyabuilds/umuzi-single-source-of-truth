## What is this tool?
The Google Classroom Grade Sync Tool automatically syncs student grades and assignment data from Google Classroom into a beautifully formatted Google Sheet. It helps you track student progress and identify who needs help.

- **Monitor course progress:** see how learners are doing daily
- **Identify struggling students early:** before they fall behind
- **Save hours of manual work:** no more building grade books from scratch
- **Focus on teaching:** let the tool handle the data organisation

## Getting Started 

### Step 1: Create your sheet
1. Copy the Umuzi Grade Sync Master Google Sheet and name it "[Your Name] - [Cohort] Gradebook".
2. That's it! Your copy has all the code and functionality.

### Step 2: Connect Your Classroom
1. Open your copy of the spreadsheet.
2. Wait as the page loads for the "🎓 Classroom Sync" menu to appear.
3. Click "🎓 Classroom Sync" → "🔄 Sync Grades from Classroom".
4. Select a course from the popup list.
5. Click "Sync Selected Course" - first sync takes 1-2 minutes.

### Step 3: Set Up Key Assessments
1. Click "🎓 Classroom Sync" → "🎯 Mark Summative Assignments".
2. Check the boxes for summative assessments (exams, major projects, capstones).
3. Click "Save & Update Analysis" - this turns on the risk detection.

## Multi-sheet View

### Morning Check 
1. Click "Sync Current Course": updates all grades from Classroom.
2. Scan the "At Risk" column: see who needs immediate help.
3. Check "Off Track" column: note who's falling behind.
4. Take quick action: message struggling students.

### Weekly Review

## Simple Troubleshooting

### Common Quick Fixes:
- **"No Google Classroom courses found"**
  - Make sure you're a teacher in the Google Classroom.
  - Check you're logged into the right Google account.
  
- **Menu not appearing**
  - Refresh the spreadsheet.
  - Wait 30 seconds after opening.
  
- **Missing assignments**
  - Assignments need due dates in Classroom to sync properly.
  
- **Error messages**
  - Try "Sync Current Course" instead of the full sync.
  - Check your internet connection.
