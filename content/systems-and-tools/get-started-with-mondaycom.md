## Managing your work on Monday.com

Stay on top of all the tasks assigned to you across your monday.com account with **My Work**. Managers, this will also stay on top of your team's workload. 😁

> **Tip:** Check My Work first thing in the morning to see upcoming deadlines. You can even edit item information using My Work, eliminating the need to jump between boards, saving you time.

### Tips to streamline your work

- Perform bulk actions 
- Manage your notifications
- **Favourites**: When working throughout monday.com, there may be some boards, dashboards, and workdocs you want easy access to. Favourites is the best way to ensure that your most-used content is always handy!
