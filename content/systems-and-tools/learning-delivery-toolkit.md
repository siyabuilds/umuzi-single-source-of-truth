## How Do I Decide Which Platform I Should Use for Learner Submissions and Review?
Answer the following questions to find the best platform for you:

1. Determine what you are trying to assess and review the options above to see if there is one that will enable the kind of submission you want learners to make.  
   - What are you trying to assess (coding, data sci, etc)?
  
2. Determine how the submission will be reviewed to see which ones will enable the kind of reviewing you need.  
   - Will this be human reviewed and/or AI reviewed?
  
3. How should feedback look? Score, rubric, comment, etc?  
4. How will feedback be given to learners?  
5. Double check project budget in case the preferred option is not free.
