# Briefs, Content Generation + Quality Control

## Aim
To improve the quality of DPD briefs and maintain it throughout the learner's journey and year by year.

## General

### What is a brief?
A brief is a set of instructions, requirements, sources, and tasks given to learners in the process of teaching and learning. At Umuzi, briefs are used to teach learners theory, application, and tools related to the stream the learner is in. 

Briefs can be accessed in the *DPD Team Drive > Content*.

## Set Up
Before the update process can start, the following activities need to take place to ensure that people are assigned to the task and they know what they need to do:

- Deciding on timelines and deadlines
- Assigning the person responsible for the work
- Deciding the person responsible to check progress and completion (usually the department manager)

## Creating New Briefs
There are two parts to creating new briefs:

### Process to follow when creating a brief from scratch (i.e., one that does not exist):
1. Decide on the theme and goal of the brief
2. Research content to include
3. Record sources
4. Access the template and make a new copy
5. Use the new copy to write the brief
6. Do quality control
7. Review with the department manager
