# Sprint - Assessment

## AIM
The intention of this document is to assist and guide a facilitator in the assessment of the Upskilling sprints which occur in the Foundations, Advanced, and Product Teams phase of the learner's journey called - Foundations, Advanced, and Product Teams Upskilling.

## ASSESSMENT - STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

This is a step-by-step guide for how to view the learners' submissions.

### STEP 1: SUBMISSION LINKS
First, share this link with the learners - it's the recruits' portal for uploading submissions. Then you will need to check that each learner has submitted their required links on the DPD: Cohorts Airtable base. Then navigate to the Recruits x Sprints tab.

### STEP 2: ASSESSMENT
You will be required to go on Airtable using the Recruit x Cohort tab to complete the assessing.

- Add the DPD Manager by selecting the cell, then select the + button. 
- Reach out to either Ryan or Teddy with regard to adding you as a manager.

Learners are assessed on four tiers: **Conceptual**, **Technical**, **Professionalism**, and **Presentation**. Usually, we assess their Conceptual, Technical, and Professional skills. We only assess Presentation at the end of sprint 3 since that's when they do a presentation.

> Comments are optional.

- Add the format of the assessment e.g. Individual, Team project.
- You can reach out to Ryan on how to populate Recruits x Sprints.

### STEP 3: REPEAT THE PROCESS
Repeat this process literally every week so you don't fall behind with the assessment. The data comes in handy when attending the learner review + support meeting. It's an opportunity for you to share with partner managers how learners are doing and where they need support.
